About the Conference

The University of Bari Aldo Moro is hosting the 15th edition of the conference on the “Economics of Global Interactions” on 8-9 September 2025. The conference is co-organized with the Centre for Experimental Research on Fairness, Inequality, and Rationality (FAIR) at NHH Norwegian School of Economics (Bergen, Norway) and Master EGEI within the GRINS - Growing Resilient, Inclusive and Sustainable project.

Sponsors of the event include SNF-Norway and Cerpem srl.

The event will be part of the (online) summer school in “Economics of Global Interactions” which will involve 25 students (8-10 September 2025). [Participants in the EGI Summer School will obtain 4 ECTS]

The event will provide scholars with the opportunity to discuss their recent contributions in the following areas:

  • International trade and trade policy;
  • Determinants and effects of international migration;
  • FDI and Global Value Chains;
  • Institutions and economic development;
  • Behavioural economics and development;
  • Structural change and global interactions;

A discussant will be assigned to each paper presented at the conference.

Keynote Speakers

Paola Conconi

University of Oxford

Professor of Economics (Statutory Chair) at the University of Oxford. Professorial Fellow at New College Oxford. Member of the European Center for Advanced Research in Economics and Statistics (ECARES).

Axel Dreher

Heidelberg University

Professor of International and Development Politics at Heidelberg University’s Alfred-Weber-Institute for Economics. Editor of the Review of International Organizations. Co-Director of CefES, member of the German National Academy of Sciences Leopoldina.
